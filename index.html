Education & Career Journey

My professional path has been driven by a deep fascination with cutting-edge technology and advanced engineering, particularly in designing and maintaining smart systems across diverse industries including aerospace, automotive, renewable energy, and energy management. This passion guided my academic choices, leading me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Electronics/Electrical Energy and Control Engineering in Lille, France, where I rediscovered my enthusiasm for designing electrical machines, actuators, and developing their control systems for smart applications like wind turbines, electric/hybrid vehicles, and aircraft systems.

Never content to stop learning, I immersed myself in research to stay at the forefront of technological advancements in electronics and machine design. This dedication earned me a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering and Sustainable Development, where I graduated ranked 5th in my cohort.

My relentless curiosity and commitment to contributing to advanced engineering solutions have cultivated a unique multidisciplinary expertise spanning automotive, aerospace, renewable energy, and IT— including software development, infrastructure maintenance, architectural insights, and network engineering.

This diverse technical foundation allows me to bridge gaps between traditional engineering disciplines and emerging smart technologies, delivering innovative solutions at the intersection of sustainability and high-performance systems.
